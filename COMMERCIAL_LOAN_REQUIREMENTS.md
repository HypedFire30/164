# Commercial Real Estate Loan Requirements - Data Mapping

This document outlines the customer information needed to generate each document required by InRoads Credit Union for commercial real estate loan applications.

---

## BUSINESS DOCUMENTS

### 1. 2024 Business Tax Returns (State and Federal) including ALL schedules and K-1s

**Information Needed:**

#### Business Entity Information
- Business name (legal name)
- EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- Business address (street, city, state, zip)
- Entity type (LLC, Corporation, Partnership, S-Corp, etc.)
- State of incorporation/formation
- Tax year end date
- Filing status (e.g., calendar year, fiscal year)

#### Business Income (Schedule C, E, F, or Form 1120/1120S/1065)
- Gross receipts/sales
- Cost of goods sold (if applicable)
- Gross profit
- Business expenses by category:
  - Advertising
  - Car and truck expenses
  - Commissions and fees
  - Contract labor
  - Depreciation and Section 179 expense
  - Employee benefit programs
  - Insurance
  - Interest (mortgage, business loans)
  - Legal and professional services
  - Office expenses
  - Pension and profit-sharing plans
  - Rent or lease payments
  - Repairs and maintenance
  - Supplies
  - Taxes and licenses
  - Travel, meals, and entertainment
  - Utilities
  - Wages
  - Other expenses (with descriptions)

#### Business Assets (for depreciation schedules)
- Asset description
- Date placed in service
- Cost basis
- Depreciation method (MACRS, straight-line, etc.)
- Depreciation taken in current year
- Accumulated depreciation
- Current year depreciation deduction

#### Business Liabilities
- Business loans and notes payable
- Accounts payable
- Accrued expenses
- Other business debts

#### K-1 Information (if Partnership/LLC/S-Corp)
- Partnership/LLC name and EIN
- Partner/member name and SSN/EIN
- Ownership percentage
- Share of income/loss
- Share of deductions
- Share of credits
- Distributions received
- Capital contributions
- Basis information
- At-risk amounts
- Passive activity information

#### Business Tax Payments
- Estimated tax payments made
- Tax withheld
- Refund or amount owed

---

### 2. Completed Business Debt Schedule (InRoads provided form)

**Information Needed:**

For each business debt/loan:
- Creditor/lender name
- Account number (last 4 digits)
- Type of debt (term loan, line of credit, equipment loan, etc.)
- Original loan amount
- Current outstanding balance
- Interest rate (annual percentage rate)
- Payment frequency (monthly, quarterly, etc.)
- Monthly/annual payment amount
- Payment due date
- Maturity date
- Collateral description (if secured)
- Personal guarantee (yes/no)
- Co-signer information (if applicable)
- Current status (current, past due, etc.)
- Days past due (if applicable)
- Purpose of loan
- Relationship to business (business loan, personal loan used for business, etc.)

---

### 3. Interim Profit & Loss and Income Statement

**Information Needed:**

#### Revenue/Income (for the interim period)
- Gross rental income (by property)
- Other rental income (parking, storage, etc.)
- Service income
- Interest income
- Dividend income
- Other income sources (with descriptions)
- Total revenue

#### Expenses (for the interim period)
- Property management fees
- Maintenance and repairs
- Property taxes
- Insurance
- Utilities
- Professional fees (legal, accounting)
- Marketing/advertising
- Office expenses
- Payroll and benefits
- Interest expense
- Depreciation
- Amortization
- Other expenses (with descriptions)
- Total expenses

#### Net Income
- Gross profit (Revenue - Cost of Goods Sold, if applicable)
- Operating income
- Net income before taxes
- Net income after taxes

#### Time Period Information
- Statement period start date
- Statement period end date
- Comparison period (prior year same period, if applicable)
- Year-to-date information

---

### 4. Current Leases (on apartment buildings)

**Information Needed:**

For each lease:
- Property address
- Unit number/identifier
- Tenant name
- Tenant contact information (phone, email)
- Lease start date
- Lease end date
- Lease term (months)
- Monthly rent amount
- Security deposit amount
- Pet deposit (if applicable)
- Parking fee (if separate)
- Storage fee (if separate)
- Other fees (application fees, etc.)
- Renewal options (automatic renewal, option to renew, etc.)
- Renewal terms (rent increase, etc.)
- Lease type (fixed-term, month-to-month, etc.)
- Special terms or conditions
- Guarantor information (if applicable)
- Lease document (file attachment)
- Current status (active, expired, terminated)
- Last payment date
- Payment history (on-time, late, etc.)

---

### 5. Current Rent Roll

**Information Needed:**

For each unit:
- Property address
- Unit number/identifier
- Unit type (studio, 1BR, 2BR, etc.)
- Square footage
- Number of bedrooms
- Number of bathrooms
- Tenant name
- Lease start date
- Lease end date
- Monthly rent amount
- Security deposit
- Other monthly charges (parking, storage, utilities, etc.)
- Total monthly rent
- Occupancy status (occupied, vacant, under renovation)
- Move-in date
- Move-out date (if vacant)
- Days vacant (if applicable)
- Market rent (for comparison)
- Last rent increase date
- Last rent increase amount
- Payment status (current, past due, etc.)
- Days past due (if applicable)
- Tenant contact information

**Summary Information:**
- Total units
- Occupied units
- Vacant units
- Occupancy rate
- Total monthly rent (potential)
- Total monthly rent (collected)
- Average rent per unit
- Average rent per square foot

## PERSONAL DOCUMENTS

### 6. 2024 Personal Tax Returns (State and Federal) including ALL schedules and K-1s

**Information Needed:**

#### Personal Information
- Full legal name
- Social Security Number (SSN)
- Date of birth
- Filing status (Single, Married Filing Jointly, Married Filing Separately, Head of Household, Qualifying Widow(er))
- Spouse name and SSN (if married filing jointly)
- Address (street, city, state, zip)
- Dependents (name, SSN, relationship, age)

#### Income (W-2, 1099, Schedule K-1, etc.)
- W-2 income:
  - Employer name and EIN
  - Wages, tips, other compensation
  - Federal income tax withheld
  - Social Security tax withheld
  - Medicare tax withheld
  - State income tax withheld
  - Local income tax withheld
  - Retirement plan contributions
  - Health insurance premiums
- 1099 income:
  - Interest income (1099-INT)
  - Dividend income (1099-DIV)
  - Miscellaneous income (1099-MISC)
  - Non-employee compensation (1099-NEC)
  - Retirement distributions (1099-R)
  - Other 1099 forms
- Schedule K-1 income (from partnerships, LLCs, S-Corps, trusts, estates)
- Rental income (Schedule E)
- Business income (Schedule C)
- Capital gains/losses (Schedule D)
- Other income sources

#### Deductions
- Standard deduction OR itemized deductions:
  - Medical and dental expenses
  - State and local taxes (SALT)
  - Real estate taxes
  - Personal property taxes
  - Mortgage interest
  - Charitable contributions
  - Casualty and theft losses
  - Other itemized deductions
- Above-the-line deductions:
  - Educator expenses
  - Student loan interest
  - HSA contributions
  - Traditional IRA contributions
  - Self-employed health insurance
  - Self-employed SEP, SIMPLE, and qualified plans
  - Self-employment tax deduction
  - Other adjustments

#### Credits
- Child tax credit
- Earned income credit
- Education credits
- Retirement savings contributions credit
- Other credits

#### Tax Payments
- Estimated tax payments
- Tax withheld from W-2s
- Refund or amount owed

#### K-1 Information (Personal)
- Partnership/LLC/S-Corp/Trust/Estate name and EIN
- Partner/member/beneficiary name and SSN
- Ownership percentage
- Share of income/loss
- Share of deductions
- Share of credits
- Distributions received
- Capital contributions
- Basis information
- At-risk amounts
- Passive activity information

### 8. Personal Finance Statement (InRoads provided form)

**Information Needed:**

#### Personal Information
- Full name
- Date of birth
- SSN
- Address (street, city, state, zip)
- Phone number
- Email address
- Marital status
- Spouse name (if applicable)
- Spouse SSN (if applicable)
- Number of dependents

#### Assets

**Liquid Assets:**
- Cash on hand
- Checking accounts (bank name, account number, balance)
- Savings accounts (bank name, account number, balance)
- Money market accounts (bank name, account number, balance)
- Certificates of deposit (bank name, account number, balance, maturity date, interest rate)

**Investments:**
- Stocks (company name, number of shares, current value)
- Bonds (issuer, face value, current value, maturity date, interest rate)
- Mutual funds (fund name, number of shares, current value)
- ETFs (fund name, number of shares, current value)
- Retirement accounts (401k, IRA, Roth IRA - account type, custodian, current value)
- Other investments (description, current value)

**Real Estate:**
- Primary residence (address, current market value, mortgage balance, equity)
- Investment properties (address, current market value, mortgage balance, equity, rental income)
- Commercial properties (address, current market value, mortgage balance, equity, rental income)
- Land (address, current market value, any liens)
- Other real estate (description, current market value, mortgage balance, equity)

**Personal Property:**
- Vehicles (make, model, year, current value, loan balance)
- Boats, RVs, etc. (description, current value, loan balance)
- Jewelry, art, collectibles (description, appraised value)
- Other personal property (description, estimated value)

**Business Interests:**
- Business ownership (business name, ownership percentage, estimated value)
- Partnership interests (partnership name, ownership percentage, estimated value)
- Other business interests (description, estimated value)

**Other Assets:**
- Life insurance cash value (policy type, face value, cash surrender value)
- Notes receivable (debtor name, original amount, current balance, interest rate)
- Other assets (description, estimated value)

**Total Assets**

#### Liabilities

**Real Estate Loans:**
- Primary residence mortgage (lender, original amount, current balance, monthly payment, interest rate, maturity date)
- Investment property mortgages (property address, lender, original amount, current balance, monthly payment, interest rate, maturity date)
- Commercial property mortgages (property address, lender, original amount, current balance, monthly payment, interest rate, maturity date)
- Home equity loans/lines (lender, current balance, monthly payment, interest rate, credit limit)

**Personal Loans:**
- Auto loans (lender, original amount, current balance, monthly payment, interest rate, maturity date, collateral)
- Student loans (lender, original amount, current balance, monthly payment, interest rate, maturity date)
- Personal loans (lender, original amount, current balance, monthly payment, interest rate, maturity date, purpose)
- Other loans (description, lender, current balance, monthly payment, interest rate)

**Credit Cards:**
- Credit card accounts (issuer, card name, credit limit, current balance, minimum monthly payment, interest rate)

**Lines of Credit:**
- Personal lines of credit (institution, credit limit, current balance, available credit, interest rate)
- Business lines of credit (institution, credit limit, current balance, available credit, interest rate)

**Other Liabilities:**
- Accounts payable
- Taxes owed
- Other debts (description, creditor, current balance, monthly payment)

**Total Liabilities**

#### Income

**Employment Income:**
- Salary/wages (employer, annual amount, monthly amount)
- Bonuses (employer, annual amount)
- Commissions (employer, annual amount)
- Other employment income (description, annual amount)

**Rental Income:**
- Property rental income (property address, monthly amount, annual amount)

**Investment Income:**

**Business Income:**

**Other Income:**

- Other income (description, annual amount)

## SUMMARY: Data Model Additions Needed

Based on the requirements above, your current data model would need the following additions/enhancements:

### New Entities/Fields Needed:

1. **Tax Returns (Business & Personal)**
   - Tax year
   - Filing status
   - Income by category
   - Expenses by category
   - Deductions
   - Credits
   - Tax payments
   - Refund/amount owed
   - K-1 information

2. **Business Debt Schedule**
   - Enhanced business loan tracking with all required fields

3. **Profit & Loss Statement**
   - Time period tracking
   - Revenue by category
   - Expenses by category
   - Comparison periods

4. **Leases**
   - Complete lease information
   - Tenant information
   - Payment history
   - Renewal options

5. **Rent Roll**
   - Unit-level information
   - Occupancy tracking
   - Market rent comparison

6. **W-2 Information**
   - Employment details
   - Tax withholding information

7. **Enhanced Personal Finance Statement**
   - More detailed asset categories
   - More detailed liability categories
   - Expense tracking
   - Contingent liabilities

### Existing Entities That Can Be Enhanced:

- **BusinessEntity**: Add tax return information, detailed debt schedule
- **RealEstateProperty**: Add lease information, rent roll data
- **PersonalInfo**: Add tax filing information, dependents
- **IncomeSource**: Add W-2 details, tax withholding
- **PersonalLoan/CreditLine/CreditCard**: Already have most needed fields
- **FullPFS**: Add expense tracking, contingent liabilities
